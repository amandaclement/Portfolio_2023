creative statement

After years of trying to characterize myself as either an analytical thinker or a creative thinker, I finally found my place in-between. This realization was brought on as I learned to code and discovered that a structured and methodical approach could lead to something abstract and expressive. Naturally, code became my preferred tool. While many of my works focus on functionality and efficiency, exploring what can emerge through experimentation is what motivates me most as the creation process relies on logical thinking and problem-solving, but the outcome is only vaguely predefined and predictable, and can be unexpectedly beautiful.
